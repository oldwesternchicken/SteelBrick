Our Services

Comprehensive bricklaying and construction solutions for all your needs

New Builds

Full structural brickwork for new homes and extensions delivered to specification and building code standards.

  • Full structural wall construction
  • Blockwork and brick cladding
  • Lintels, openings and detailing
  • Collaboration with trades and engineers

Rennovations

Skilled repair, restoration and renovation work to breathe new life into existing brick and stone structures.

  • Structural repair & repointing
  • Restoration of heritage features
  • Window/door openings & lintel replacement
  • Matching new work to existing finishes

Landscaping

Durable and attractive external brickwork and stonework solutions to enhance gardens, driveways and outdoor spaces.

  • Retaining walls & garden walls
  • Paving, steps and edging
  • BBQ areas, raised planters and features
  • Drainage-friendly construction
